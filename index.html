Service & Volunteerism

 

I want to give back to the community!

Giving back to the community is important and Stout has many ways to help you make a difference. Check out various short or long term volunteer opportunities, go by yourself or start a volunteer team with a few friends, or take part in experiential learning through one of your courses for credit. You can also connect with a group of students who are dedicated to service through a student organization. All of these options provide significant learning experiences for students through meaningful service activities that benefit the community.

Stoutreach: Stoutreach is the campus volunteer program coordinated by Ally Initiatives in the Involvement Center. There are currently over 30 local agencies and opportunities waiting to meet you and seeking your help!

I'm not sure how to find my one...

Not sure how to find your one? Or not sure how involved you want to be yet? That’s OK! We’re here to help!

Sign up for a Find Your Fit Session: Meet one-on-one with a student Involvement Specialist to discuss your interests, set goals and explore opportunities to get involved. Together, we can find the very best fit for you! Follow the link to sign up for a session.

Attend a Campus Event: There is always something going on around campus. Whether it’s a student org event, a Casino Night hosted by the Involvement Center or a fitness class at University Recreation, attending a campus event might just help you find your one! View upcoming events to the right or follow the link to view all campus events.

Search the Student Org Directory: With over 160 student organizations, it may take a bit of searching to find the one that’s right for you! Browse by alpha order, category or keyword. Chances are we’ll have something that you’ll love!

Start a New Organization: Do you have an interest in something and don’t see a related student organization? Students are always starting up new organizations! The Stout Student Assocation reviews and approves all requests for new student organizations. If you’re interested in bringing a certain interest to UW-Stout, visit the student org website for information on the requirements and process online today!
